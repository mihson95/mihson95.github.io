Profile Summary

I am a Master's Student in Software Engineering and Data Analytics at University of Limerick. I have a demonstrated history of leveraging innovative engineering methodologies to drive organizational success.

Background

Ever since high school, I have been interested in coding. In class 9, I learned HTML and designed a website, as a school project on the Effects of Global Warming. In class 12, I learned C++ in school and learned about Object-oriented programming. I loved how logic along with creativity in computer programs have made life more comfortable and brought an enormous advancement in technology. It became clear to me that I will one day build my career in the fascinating field of computers, and that determination is within me to this present day.

My nature has always inclined me toward planning ahead; it's how I've functioned from the start. During my time in high school, I started making an active effort to pursue my dream of studying at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) - one of the most prestigious technical institutes in the world. I dedicated three years of meticulous study and unwavering hard work, and it paid off. I secured an All India Rank (AIR) of 3245 to earn my place at IIT Madras. To satiate my curiosity about a computer's fundamental functioning and my desire to become an adept software developer, I decided to take elective courses in Programming in C, Data Structures, and Algorithms, Linear Algebra, and Applied Statistics. These courses have had a profound impact on my interest and have facilitated intimate knowledge and understanding of the core workings of systems.

I was drawn to mobile application development because mobile devices are easily accessible and mobile applications have an immense range of practical uses. Therefore, I decided to learn Android application development independently. I developed an interactive map application for the university to manage on-campus events. I collaborated with other talented and passionate students from various Disciplines. To work amongst like-minded people who are from different specialisations yet passionate about software was an enriching experience.

I am pursuing a postgraduate degree in Software Engineering and Data Analytics from the University of Limerick to get me closer to my professional goals and provide me with an opportunity to gain in-depth knowledge and expertise by developing projects in the fields of Software Design, Architecture, Quality and Machine Learning, Data Mining, and Big Data and also improve my Profile to join a Technology firm as a Mid-Senior Level Software Development Professional.
